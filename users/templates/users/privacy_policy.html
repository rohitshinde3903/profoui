Privacy Policy

At Stones Web Services, we value and prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our social media management platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, profile picture, and other account details.
  • Usage Data: Activities within the platform, interactions, and scheduling preferences.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver personalized social media management tools and services.
  • Improve user experience and platform functionality.
  • Send service updates, notifications, and marketing communications.
  • Ensure security, detect fraudulent activities, and maintain compliance.

3. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, gather analytics, and provide personalized content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@stoneswebservices.com.

6. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Continued use of our platform signifies your agreement to the updated policy.
